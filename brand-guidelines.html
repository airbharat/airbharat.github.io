Logo & Visual Identity

The AirBharat Symbol

Our logo represents the soaring spirit of India - dynamic wings that symbolize progress, innovation, and our commitment to connecting India with the world. The geometric design reflects precision and modernity while honoring traditional Indian craftsmanship.

Primary Logo - Light Backgrounds

Use on white or light colored backgrounds

Primary Logo - Dark Backgrounds

Use on dark or heavily colored backgrounds

‚úó DON'T

Clear Space Requirements

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "A" in "AirBharat" on all sides.

Brand Voice & Messaging

AirBharat's voice reflects the confidence, warmth, and innovation of modern India. We communicate with authority while remaining approachable, professional yet personal.

Confident

Imagery & Visual Style

Our visual style celebrates the beauty of flight, the diversity of India, and the sophistication of modern aviation. Images should be bright, optimistic, and professionally executed.

Photography Style

  • High contrast, vibrant colors
  • Natural lighting preferred
  • Human-centered storytelling
  • Clean, uncluttered compositions
  • Authentic moments over posed shots

Color Treatment

  • Emphasize warm tones during golden hour
  • Cool blues for sky and aircraft shots
  • Maintain brand color harmony
  • Avoid oversaturation
  • Consistent color grading across campaigns

Subject Matter

  • Diverse passengers from all backgrounds
  • Modern aircraft and facilities
  • Indian landscapes and destinations
  • Service moments and hospitality
  • Technology and innovation
